As a UX/product designer, I've always been captivated by the enchanting experiences Disney creates. From the immersive storytelling in their films to the seamless interactions in their theme parks, Disney's commitment to user-centric design is a constant source of inspiration. The concept of " plussing,"  or continually improving details to enhance the overall experience, resonates deeply with my approach to design. I strive to bring this same magic into my work, crafting intuitive and engaging user experiences that delight and surprise. On the side, I've developed a passion for microanimations, which allow me to infuse even the smallest interactions with a touch of Disney's whimsy. By combining my professional skills with this creative outlet, I aim to create digital experiences that are not just functional but also memorable and enchanting, much like a visit to the "Happiest Place on Earth."
